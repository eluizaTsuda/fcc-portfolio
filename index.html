My name is Luiza, but you can call me by my nickname "Luh!"

I am a web developer based on the beautiful eastern coast of Canada, St.John's.

I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in applied mathematics and computing science; and a graduate Diploma in services-oriented software engineering.

I worked as a systems analyst for several years in my home country. Now I am in a career transition, and my current goal is to acquire knowledge in web development.

I am a self-taught web developer; I have been working with the FreeCodeCamp curriculum to help me achieve this. Given my technical background, my journey has been smooth and rewarding.

In the section below, you will find some projects that I have completed so far and that I will continue to grow and, I hope, improve as I work with the curriculum.

I will be updating this site whenever I complete a new project.

I love what I do - turning ideas into reality by coding. Follow me on this journey.
